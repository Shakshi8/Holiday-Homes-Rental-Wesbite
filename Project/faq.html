Frequently Asked Questions

What do you need help with?

Account

How do I create an account?

You can sign up using the "Sign up" button on the top right of the web page

How do I update my name/email address?

You can update your name and email address by going to Account Settings - Personal Info.

Here you can update your name, gender, date of birth, email address and physical address.

How do I update my account password?

You can update your account password by going to Account Settings - Login & Security.

How do I check my previous transactions?

You can check your previous transactions by going to Account Settings - Payments & Payouts.
